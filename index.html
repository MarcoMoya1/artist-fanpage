Who was Lil Peep?

Lil Peep was a young swedish-American rapper who died in November of 2017 at the young age of 21. He was part of a rap group called "Goth boy Clique" and was on a music tour at the time of his death. He is mourned by not only his family and friends but also millions of fans worldwide.

A few well know songs by him were

  • Beamer Boy
  • 16 Lines
  • Gym Class
  • Star Shopping
  • Life is Beautiful
  • Awful Things
